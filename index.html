About Me

Hi there! I'm GC Mouli, a tech enthusiast with over 20 years of experience in product management and engineering leadership. I currently work at Microsoft and have a Ph.D. from the University of South Florida in computer engineering.

Over the years, I've had the chance to build and lead amazing global teams across B2B, B2C, and B2B2C in Large Enterprise companies and startups. I have realized in recent years that I have maximum satisfaction at the intersection of people, product, and technology.

I mentor junior product managers and coach senior startup folks. Passionate about storytelling and writing, I'm currently retelling the Tamil epic novel Ponniyin Selvan in English.

I'm super interested in company cultures, career arcs, people, behaviors, personalities, and I learn all this from meeting people over breakfast.
