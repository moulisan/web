Hi there! I'm GC Mouli, a product leader with over 20 years of experience in Product Management and Engineering Leadership.

Through my career, I've built and led successful global teams across B2B, B2C, and B2B2C, working with both large enterprises and startups. I've found that my sweet spot is at the intersection of people, product, and technology - that's where I make the biggest impact.

Outside of work, I love storytelling and am currently working on an English retelling of the Tamil epic, Ponniyin Selvan. I love to teach and instruct an occasional course on Product Management for the PGP Pro program at ISB; and facilitate workshops on storytelling and communication.

I'm always curious about what makes companies and people tick. I love learning about different company cultures (especially cross-geographic setups), career paths, and personalities. One of my favorite ways to do this is by meeting people over breakfast (search on linkedin for #dosaWithMouli). It's amazing what you can learn over a good meal!
