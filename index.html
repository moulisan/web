People, Product, Technology

"My sweet spot is at the intersection of people, product, and technology—that's where I make the biggest impact."

I'm a product leader with over twenty years in Product Management and Engineering Leadership. Through my career, I've built and led global teams across B2B, B2C, and B2B2C, working with both large enterprises and startups.

Outside of work, I'm drawn to storytelling. I'm currently writing an English retelling of Ponniyin Selvan, the Tamil epic. I teach Product Management for ISB's PGP Pro program and facilitate workshops on storytelling and communication.

I'm endlessly curious about what makes companies and people tick—different cultures, career paths, personalities. One of my favorite ways to learn is meeting people over breakfast. Search LinkedIn for #dosaWithMouli to see what I mean.
