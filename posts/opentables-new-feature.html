OpenTable's new feature

Restaurant reservation startup Open Table has a new feature and I think it is a pretty cool feature (I think!). This is rolled out only in select cities and in select restaurants. You book a restaurant reservation through OpenTable. You arrive at the restaurant. You order your meal. You finish. And then the restaurant bill arrives on your mobile phone through OpenTable. You swipe to pay direct from your phone. Done. You walk away. And yeah, ofcourse, this caught my attention of the South Indian food on display in the phone app in the picture :) And sure, it will not roll out in India for a while, but I think this is where the future lies.
