Candle light beauty

The power went off day before yesterday, and I could not help gaze at the beauty of the lone candle light at the corner of our living room. I ran in with my torchlight, grabbed my trusty nikon coolpix. Today I touched up the pic using the trusty picnik.com, and voila, here I present to you ' Candle light beauty'.
