Three Months into my current startup gig

It is going to be 3 months (to the date, today) since I joined my current gig - the mythical beast - a consumer AI startup in stealth. Early stage startups are all about excitement, building from scratch, seeing things come up from nothing, users finding joy in using what you build, and just the sheer adrenaline rush. Startups are also about chaos, extra long hours, the push towards shipping, looking at every early users behavior, iterating and refining, and just, well, the same adrenaline rush.

This is not for everybody. And only those who enjoy this, will thrive. And no, I am not advocating for everyone to do 70 hours or 7 days/wk or whatever the latest meme is about. It is about balancing your exertion, with rest and breaks. And then you do it all over again.
